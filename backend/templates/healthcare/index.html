About Meridian

Trusted By
Generations.

Founded in 1948, Meridian Health has grown from a single community hospital to a comprehensive health system serving over 1 million patients annually. Our mission is simple: provide the highest quality care with compassion and respect.

We invest over $100M annually in medical technology and research, ensuring our patients have access to the latest treatments and clinical trials.
