Our Mission

We believe every person deserves access to clean water, quality education, and economic opportunity.

Founded in 2008, Horizon Foundation has grown from a small grassroots initiative into one of the world's most effective and transparent nonprofits. We partner with local communities to build lasting solutions — not temporary fixes.

Our model is simple: hire local, train local, sustain local. Every project we build is maintained by the community it serves, ensuring impact that lasts for decades.
