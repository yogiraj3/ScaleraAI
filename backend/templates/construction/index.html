About Ironclad

Built on Integrity.
Driven by Precision.

Founded in 1992, Ironclad Construction has grown from a small residential contractor into one of the region's most trusted general construction firms. We've completed over 500 projects totaling $2.4 billion in value — from single-family homes to 50-storey commercial towers.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to safety, quality, and transparent communication. Every project gets a dedicated project manager, real-time reporting dashboards, and our ironclad guarantee: on time, on budget, or we make it right.
